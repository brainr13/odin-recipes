Salmon with Lemon and Dill

This is a simple dish where the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. It's simple to make, simple to cook, and tastes amazing!

The dish can be made any time of the year. However, in Summer it's even better with fresh dill and fress lemon. Plus, it's a great dish to make for summer parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon filets
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 5 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • sea salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a medium baking dish.
  2. Place salmon in the baking dish. Mix the butter and lemon juice in a small bowl, and drizzle over the salmon.
  3. Season with dill, garlic powder, sea salt, and pepper.
  4. Bake 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until salmon is easily flaked with a fork.
